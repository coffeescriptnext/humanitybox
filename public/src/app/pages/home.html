Humanity Box is a free ad network designed to increase exposure to individuals raising funds for personal medical needs.

Install on your website »

Ads that change lives.

Humanity Box works similarly to how paid ad networks work (such as The Deck, Carbon Ads, BSA One, etc). You install an ad code onto your website, and it delivers advertisements on each page load.

The difference is that the ads in the Humanity Box network are funding campaigns for people experiencing financial hardship due to medical reasons. The campaigns are sourced from our partner, GiveForward.

We rotate 10 campaigns per day. After each 24-hour period, a new set of 10 campaigns are put into rotation. Our goal is to bring exposure to as many hard-to-find campaigns as possible.

Why does it exist?

Our partner, GiveForward, is providing a vital service by allowing people to create funding campaigns to pay for their loved ones' medical treatments. To date, more than $100 million has been given on the platform.

Oftentimes, however, the campaigns never reach beyond friends, family, and other people specifically browsing GiveForward looking to make donations. While this is amazing in and of itself, many campaigns never reach their goal. Humanity Box's aim is to expose these campaigns to as many sites on the internet as possible. By increasing traffic to these campaigns we can help them get funded when they otherwise might not.
